Lionel Messi scored 1 goal and 1 assist for Argentina at the 2006 FIFA World Cup. In his first World Cup appearance, Messi came on as a substitute against Serbia and Montenegro, scoring his first World Cup goal by providing an assist to Hernan Crespo. In addition, Messi won the Man of the Match award at the 2006 World Cup. Argentina reached the Quarter-Finals of the 2006 World Cup, where they lost on penalties to Germany.
